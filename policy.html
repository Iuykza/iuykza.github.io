Private Policy

tl;dr We respect your privacy. Basic personal info is used to help employees identify each other on the schedule. Also everyone's info and usage is used anonymously in statistics to improve the site itself.

Opt-out?

If you are not comfortable sharing your personal information you can op out of these services by clicking below. We will not archive anything. (This isn't a Facebook delete.) We'll wipe your data from our servers and in no way can it be restored. This deletes everything but basic schedule information -- other employees may need to know this. If you are concerned about your privacy and these two buttons don't help, I would be happy to listen.

How your information is shared

To the public
The full schedule is available without logging in. This allows a fast access method for checking the schedule from any PC without worrying about entering in a password. If anyone has a concern with this let me know.
To other employees
Your name and general schedule information are publicly shared with other employees. This helps identify who is who. Otherwise it would be really hard to swap shifts with someone if you couldn't know their schedules.
To managers
Managers get a bit more information. In addition to seeing the same data as employees, managers have a separate statistics page and control over the trades. The statistics page does identify employees by name and ranks them on trade data. This is to help shape the schedule to help floor employees in turn:
  • This person is giving away lots of shifts => we should schedule less for him.
  • This person only gives away concession shifts => we shouldn't schedule him for that.
  • This person only gives away shifts on the weekend => we shouldn't schedule those days.
To improve the site
Your usage of the website including time spent on a page, visit count, and errors are kept. When an error occurs on the back-end we'll keep a log of the error you got. This error log is along the lines of, "couldn't read thing at line 23."

How your information is not shared

To third parties
No information from Shiftswap is shared with any third-parties. No external web analytics softwares are used such as Google Analytics. Additionally web crawlers and bots are disallowed from viewing the site.
To the admin
Even the admin, who controls the website, cannot see some of your more personal data: password, email, statistics. These are encrypted within the database and require your unique password to get in.
Your contact information
This includes your phone number and email, we keep all of this to ourselves. When people want to swap shifts with you, our server will act as a middle-man. They contact the server and the server contacts you. This prevents your contact information from being accessed. Management however can see your phone number because sometimes when swapping shifts things happen.

When we collect information

Name, schedule, role
This is publicly gathered from the Kronos API and is already on file.
Email, phone number
When you sign up to receive the schedule, contact information is collected from this written form.
Age, sex, race, ...
When opting into our statistics program (see below).

Statistics Program

Shiftswap gathers statistics to determine equality in the workplace, company health, and some other great stuff. We look at things such as age, sex, religion, and disability. All of this is aggregated into graphs and charts, so you cannot be personally identified by the information you give. A small amount of information dealing with the schedule, trade data, and site usage is already apart of the statistics. Everything else requires the user to opt into our statistics program and manually enter this information. Otherwise no other information is collected without your consent for the program.
