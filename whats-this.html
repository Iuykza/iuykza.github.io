What's This?

Shiftswap is a scheduling application here to help ease the difficulty of swapping shifts between employees. No more digging for people's phone numbers, finding off-days, and determining work preferences. Shiftswap does all that.

How a swap works

  1. Shiftswap pulls schedule data from Kronos.
  2. This data is displayed in a fancy table.
  3. You tell Shiftswap you need a day off. It goes and finds available employees.
  4. Shiftswap texts them about your shift.
  5. An employee responds back and says he can do it.
  6. Shiftswap declines any other takers, saying the shift has been filled.
  7. The affected manager is informed of the shift change.
